Why does debt have a silent b?

The word debt entered the English language via Old French, and was originally spelled "dette".

Medieval and renaissance scholars later felt that the English word should reflect its Latin origins more accurately, and decided to add a b to the spelling to make it look more like its Latin equivalent debitum.

However, although the spelling changed in written texts, people countinued to pronounce it exactly as they had done before, true to its French origins.
