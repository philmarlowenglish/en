Why does debt have a silent b?

Is today the day you learned that the word debt has a silent b?

Debt entered the English language via Old French, and was originally spelled "dette".

Medieval and renaissance scholars later felt that the English word should reflect its Latin origins more accurately, and decided to add a b to debt to make it look more like the Latin word debitum.

However, although the spelling changed in written texts, people countinued to pronounce it as they had before, true to its French origins.
